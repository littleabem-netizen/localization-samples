Ph: Yes, that's correct. This medication is called Mounjaro.

Ke: How do I use it?

Ph: You inject this medication once a week into your abdomen or thigh

Ke: So I only need to use it once a week?

Ph: Yes, that's correct.

Before using it, please wash your hands. Also, clean the injection site         with an alcohol swab.
Then, remove the gray cap.

Ke: Okay.

Ph: Please the clear base of the pen firmly against your skin.
Turn the green lock ring until it stop to unlock the devise.

Ke: Okay.

Ph:  Press the purple injection button and keep holding it in place.

Ke: 1 heard a click.

Ph: That's right.

When you hear the second click, the injection is complete.

Ke: How should I store the unused pens?

Ph: Please store unused pens in the refrigerator.
Avoid placing them near the cooling vent to prevent freezing.

Ke: What side effects should I expect?

Ph: Common side effects include nausea and indigestion.
These symptoms are usually mild may improve over time.
If the symptoms become severe, please contact your doctor.
